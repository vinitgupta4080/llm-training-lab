### Multilingual prediction recorded 2026-07-19

- Training distribution: English-heavy repeated `banana` / `bandana` corpus.
- Fixed merge budget: 20.
- Learner prediction: `🤖🤖` receives the largest token-count reduction because the emoji repeats.
- Competing hypothesis: English receives the largest reduction because emoji byte pairs never
  occur in the English-only training corpus, so repetition at evaluation time cannot create a
  merge rule retroactively.

## Design

- Independent variable: number of learned merges.
- Controls: corpus, starting byte vocabulary, tie-break rule, and evaluation text.
- Metrics: vocabulary size, token count, compression ratio, round-trip correctness.
- Required slices: training-domain text and text from a different script/domain.
